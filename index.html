FieldFlow Project Management Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 5, 2026

FieldFlow Project Management ("FieldFlow", "we", "our", or "us") is a construction project management app for contractor teams. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

Information We Collect

FieldFlow may collect and process the following information:

  • Account details such as name, email address, login credentials, and authentication tokens.
  • Company, workspace, project, task, schedule, meeting, approval, report, and finance data entered by users.
  • Comments, messages, mentions, uploaded files, images, videos, voice notes, documents, and other attachments that users choose to add.
  • Optional contact details selected by a user when using contact-related features, such as adding subcontractors or external contacts.
  • Device and app information needed for push notifications, security, diagnostics, crash reporting, and service reliability.

Permissions

FieldFlow asks for permissions only when needed for app features. These may include microphone access for voice notes, camera or media access for photo and video uploads, file access for attachments, contacts access for optional contact selection, and notification permission for project and task updates.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide project management, scheduling, task, approval, reporting, meeting, notification, and collaboration features.
  • Authenticate users and protect accounts, companies, workspaces, projects, and roles.
  • Store and retrieve user-submitted project content and attachments.
  • Send relevant push notifications and in-app updates.
  • Monitor app reliability, diagnose crashes, fix bugs, and improve performance.
  • Respond to support, security, privacy, or account-deletion requests.

Sharing and Service Providers

We do not sell personal information. We may share or process information with service providers that help operate FieldFlow, including backend hosting, database/storage providers, Firebase services, push notification services, crash diagnostics, and other infrastructure providers. We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect users, or to protect the security and integrity of the service.

Data Storage and Security

FieldFlow stores app data on secure backend systems and may store limited cached data on the user's device. We use technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect account, project, and attachment data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

Data Retention and Deletion

We keep information for as long as needed to provide FieldFlow, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, maintain audit history, and protect the service. Users may request account deletion from the app's account settings where available. Account deletion may anonymize user identity while preserving project audit history, records, and content that must remain available to other authorized company or project members.

Children

FieldFlow is intended for business and construction project use. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

International Processing

Information may be processed and stored in countries other than the user's country of residence, depending on where our service providers operate. By using FieldFlow, users understand that their information may be transferred and processed as described in this policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app, legal requirements, or service providers change. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions, account deletion help, or data requests, please contact the developer using the contact email shown on the Google Play listing for FieldFlow Project Management.
