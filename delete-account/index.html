Delete Your FieldFlow Account

Effective date: June 5, 2026

This page explains how users of FieldFlow Project Management can request account deletion and what happens to associated data.

Delete Your Account in the App

  1. Open FieldFlow Project Management.
  2. Sign in to the account you want to delete.
  3. Open account settings.
  4. Select the account deletion option.
  5. Confirm the deletion request when prompted.

Request Help With Deletion

If you cannot access your account, contact the developer using the contact email shown on the Google Play listing for FieldFlow Project Management. Include the email address used for the FieldFlow account so we can locate the account and help with deletion.

Data Deleted or Anonymized

When account deletion is completed, FieldFlow deletes or anonymizes personal account information such as the user's name, email address, login credentials, authentication tokens, and profile identifiers.

Data That May Be Kept

FieldFlow is used by companies and project teams. Some project records may be retained after account deletion when needed for company records, project continuity, audit history, legal compliance, dispute resolution, safety, security, or fraud prevention. This may include project and task history, approvals, schedules, comments, attachments, meeting records, finance records, and audit logs that are associated with a company or project.

Where possible, retained records are no longer linked to personally identifying account details after deletion or anonymization.

Retention Period

Account identity data is deleted or anonymized within a reasonable period after the request is verified and processed. Company and project records may be retained for as long as needed by the company workspace, legal obligations, backups, audit requirements, or service security needs.
